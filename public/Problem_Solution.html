⚠️ Section 1 — Current Problems

What Hotels Struggle With Today

Without a dedicated analytics platform, hotel operations teams face these persistent challenges every day.

📋

Paper-Based Feedback Collection

Feedback forms are filled on paper, collected manually, and often never analyzed. Insights are lost before anyone even reads them.

🔍

No Real-Time Visibility

Hotels have no way to know a department is underperforming until weeks later — by which time the guest has already complained publicly.

📊

Scattered & Siloed Data

Feedback for Room, F&B, Spa, and Pool are tracked in different spreadsheets by different teams, making comparison impossible.

⏱️

Time-Consuming Manual Reporting

Department heads spend hours each week collating feedback into PowerPoint slides for management meetings — time better spent on service improvement.

🔔

No Alert System for Low Ratings

When a guest gives a 1-star rating, no one is notified in real time. The issue festers. The guest leaves without resolution.

🔐

Data Accessibility Issues

Department heads either see too much or too little. There is no controlled, role-based access to relevant data for each team.
