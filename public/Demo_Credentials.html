Demo Environment Notice

This is a pre-configured demo environment with synthetic data. All credentials are for demonstration purposes only. Do not use these credentials in a production environment. The data reflects realistic hotel guest feedback scores and trends.

Tips for Demo Users
📊

Exploring Analytics

After logging in, use the category tabs (Room, F&B, Pool, Spa) at the top to switch between departments. The sidebar lists all composites and questions — click any to see its chart.

📅

Changing Date Ranges

Use the Weekly / Monthly / 1 Year / Custom buttons to filter data. Use the left/right arrows to navigate year-by-year. Switch to 2025 to see the full trend from low to high.

📋

Viewing Reports

Log in as manager or admin, click the ⚙️ Management icon in the header, and navigate to Yes/No Responses or Low Rating Reports.

🔀

Testing Role Restrictions

Log in as depthead to see how department-scoped access works — only Room and F&B categories appear. Log in as grand_fbhead to see single-category restriction.
