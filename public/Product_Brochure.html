Product Overview

One Platform for Your
Entire Hotel Operation

Kalalak Insight is a SaaS analytics platform designed to help hotel management teams continuously monitor guest satisfaction across every department — and act on that data in real time, not weeks later.

📊

Centralized Analytics

Every department — Rooms, Food & Beverage, Pool, Spa — is tracked in a single, beautiful dashboard. No more switching between spreadsheets or waiting for monthly reports.

📱

Frictionless Guest Feedback

Guests scan a unique QR code and submit their feedback from their smartphone in under 2 minutes. No app download. No paper form. No lost feedback.

🔔

Instant Low-Rating Alerts

When a guest scores 3 or below, an instant alert goes to your management team — so issues are resolved before check-out. When a guest scores 4 or above, they're shown your Google Review link to turn satisfaction into public reputation.

🔐

Department-Level RBAC

Assign team members to exactly what they need to see. Your F&B Head sees only F&B data. Your General Manager sees everything. Zero information overload.

📥

One-Click PDF Reports

Any chart or report in the system can be exported as a formatted PDF instantly. Management meetings become more focused, factual, and efficient.

⭐

Automatic Google Review Growth

Happy guests (rating ≥ 4) are automatically prompted with your Google Review link immediately after submitting feedback — converting in-house satisfaction into public five-star reviews that drive new bookings.
