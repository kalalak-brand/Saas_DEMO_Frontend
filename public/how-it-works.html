The Complete Journey

Every service request follows this seamless 7-step lifecycle — from guest request to management insight.

📱

Guest Scans QR Code

A unique QR code is placed in each hotel room. When a guest needs anything — towels, cleaning, room service — they simply scan the code with their phone camera. No app download required.

Engineered for Excellence

Every feature is designed to eliminate operational blind spots and delight guests.

⚡

Real-Time Notifications

WebSocket + Push Notifications ensure staff are alerted within milliseconds of a new request. Zero delay.

⏱️

30-Min SLA Escalation

Requests not picked up within 30 minutes automatically escalate. A background cron job monitors every pending request.

📈

Department Analytics

Completion rates, avg response times, and satisfaction scores broken down by Housekeeping, F&B, Front Office, and more.

🔒

Multi-Tenant Isolation

Organization → Hotel → Department → User. Every query is scoped. Your data is always isolated and secure.

📱

QR Code System

Generate room-specific and category-specific QR codes from the admin panel. Guests need zero setup.

⚠️

Low Rating Alerts

Service ratings ≤ 3 stars create internal alerts visible only to management. Resolve issues before they escalate publicly.
