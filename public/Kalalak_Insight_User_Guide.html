Instant Service Recovery Alerts

In a luxury hospitality environment, resolving guest issues before they check out is critical. Kalalak Insight includes an automated Service Recovery Alert system that notifies your team the moment a sub-optimal rating is submitted.

🚨 Immediate Action Triggered:

If a guest submits an overall rating of 3 stars or below, the system bypasses the Google Review prompt and instantly triggers an alert to the designated Department Head or Duty Manager. This allows your team to proactively reach out, apologize, and correct the issue while the guest is still on the property.

How Alerts are Delivered

To ensure no feedback slips through the cracks, alerts can be routed through multiple channels:

  • Dashboard Notifications: Real-time visual alerts appear on the main dashboard for staff actively monitoring the system.
  • Email Routing: Instant emails sent directly to the relevant department leaders containing the score, specific composite breakdowns, and any guest comments.
  • Escalation Logs: Tracked within the system so the General Manager can verify that the Duty Manager successfully closed the loop on the guest's complaint.
💡 Pro Tip: Ensure your notification routing is set up in the Settings tab so that Room Service complaints go directly to the F&B Manager, while cleanliness issues route instantly to Executive Housekeeping.

What is Kalalak Insight?

Kalalak Insight is a hotel experience intelligence platform that helps hotels understand guest satisfaction at the department level. Instead of relying on generic online reviews, Kalalak Insight collects structured, real-time feedback from guests — right when they interact with each department.

📱

QR-Based Collection

Guests scan a QR code placed in their room or common area to submit feedback instantly — no app download needed.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Ratings and trends appear on the dashboard immediately. Track performance by day, week, month, or year.

🏨

Department-Level Insight

Each department (Room, F&B, Spa, etc.) has its own set of questions and composites for focused analysis.

📄

PDF Reports

Export detailed analytics to PDF with a single click. Share with stakeholders in a professional format.

Logging In

  1. Open the Dashboard — Navigate to your hotel's Kalalak Insight dashboard URL (e.g., https://insight.kalalak.com or https://insight.kalalak.com for demo)
  2. Enter Credentials — Type your username and password on the login page
  3. Click "Sign In" — You'll be redirected to the main dashboard showing today's overview
🔒 Note: Sessions expire automatically for security. If you're logged out, simply sign in again. Your data is always safe and persisted.

Understanding Analytics

📊 Analytics Page

The analytics page is the core of Kalalak Insight. Here's what each section shows:

  • Date Range Filter — Select any time period: today, this week, this month, or a custom range
  • Question Averages — See the average rating for each question over the selected period
  • Composite Scores — Composite scores combine multiple related questions into a single metric (e.g., "Cleanliness Score" averages room cleanliness + bed/linen quality)
  • Trend Charts — Line charts showing how scores change over time
  • Yes/No Breakdown — For yes/no questions, see the percentage of "Yes" vs "No" answers
  • Staff Performance — Track which staff members contribute to positive/negative feedback
📌 Demo Data: The demo hotel includes 12 months of realistic data (Jan 2025 – Jan 2026) for the Room Service department. Explore the analytics to see seasonal patterns — notice how scores dip during monsoon months (Jun-Jul) and peak during the holiday season (Nov-Dec).

🔍 What are Composites?

Composites group related questions into meaningful scores. The demo has 3 composites:

Composite Questions Combined Purpose
Cleanliness Score Room cleanliness + Bed/linen quality (2 Qs) Overall cleanliness perception
Comfort & Amenities Bathroom + AC + In-room amenities (3 Qs) Physical comfort level
Overall Room Experience Cleanliness + Bed + Bathroom + AC (4 Qs) Holistic room quality metric

Managing Questions & Composites

❓ Questions (Super Admin Only)

Navigate to Questions in the sidebar to manage the survey questions guests see:

  • Add new questions — Choose between Rating (1–5 stars) or Yes/No type
  • Reorder questions — Drag to change the order they appear in the guest form
  • Edit text — Update question wording at any time
  • Activate/Deactivate — Hide a question without deleting it (preserves historical data)

📊 Composites (Super Admin Only)

Navigate to Composites to create grouped scores:

  1. Click "Add Composite" — Give it a descriptive name (e.g., "Guest Comfort Index")
  2. Select questions — Pick 2 or more rating questions to group together
  3. Save — The composite score will now appear in analytics as an average of the selected questions
8

Generating Reports

Kalalak Insight lets you export analytics as a professional PDF report.

  1. Go to Analytics — Select the department and date range you want to report on
  2. Click "Export PDF" — Found in the top-right corner of the analytics page
  3. Download — A formatted PDF with charts, averages, and trends will download automatically
📄 Report Includes: Question-level averages, composite scores, trend charts, rating distribution, yes/no breakdown, and guest comment highlights — all for the selected date range.

User Roles Explained

Role Access Level Can Do
Super Admin Full access Everything — manage questions, composites, users, settings, view analytics, export reports
Admin Management access View analytics, export reports, manage some settings. Cannot add/remove users or questions
Viewer Read-only View dashboard and analytics only. Ideal for department heads and managers who need to monitor performance
🏢 Organization Structure: Your hotel belongs to an organization (e.g., "Kalalak Hotels"). The SaaS Admin (platform level) manages organizations and hotels. Within each hotel, the Super Admin manages the day-to-day dashboard.
