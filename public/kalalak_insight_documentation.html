Hotel Department Efficiency Analyzer

A real-time, data-driven guest feedback platform designed specifically for the hospitality sector to measure, track, and act upon department-level performance metrics.

What is Kalalak Insight?

Kalalak Insight is an enterprise-grade Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform tailored for the hospitality industry (Hotels, Resorts, Spa, F&B). It bridges the critical gap between guest experience and management visibility.

In essence, it is an advanced dynamic feedback engine combined with a real-time analytics dashboard. Guests provide feedback via dynamically generated QR codes placed in specific hotel departments (e.g., Rooms, Restaurant, Pool). That data flows instantly into a centralized dashboard where management can view composite scores, track trends, and receive instant alerts for negative experiences before the guest checks out.

The Problem (Without Insight)

Traditional hospitality operations suffer from severe data latency and actionable blind spots when relying on standard review collection methods.

Delayed Feedback (The TripAdvisor Trap)

Guests often write negative reviews online after they check out. Management has zero opportunity to perform service recovery while the guest is on the property.

Vague, Unquantifiable Data

"The room wasn't great" is unactionable. Standard surveys do not break down metrics into specific Key Performance Indicators (e.g., 'Bed Comfort' vs. 'Bathroom Cleanliness').

Siloed Department Visibility

General Managers struggle to compare the performance of F&B against Housekeeping or the Spa because feedback is not uniformly tracked or categorized.

Missed Marketing Opportunities

Highly satisfied guests often leave without leaving an online review because there was no frictionless prompt to direct them to Google Reviews.

The Solution (With Kalalak Insight)

Kalalak Insight fundamentally changes how hotels process guest sentiment by making it immediate, departmentalized, and mathematically sound.

1

Instant Service Recovery

If a guest submits a rating of 3 stars or lower, WebSockets instantly trigger audio/visual alerts on the management dashboard, allowing staff to resolve the issue immediately.

2

Departmental Accountability

Feedback is tied directly to the QR Code scanned. If the F&B QR code is scanned, the data only affects F&B KPI metrics, making departmental troubleshooting exact.

3

Composite Indexing

Questions are grouped into "Composites" (e.g., 'Cleanliness Index', 'Food Quality Index'). This converts subjective 1-5 star ratings into hard percentage-based KPIs.

4

Smart Reputation Boosting

When a guest provides all ratings above 4 stars, the system automatically intervenes and prompts them with a frictionless link to the hotel's Google Review page.

Core Product Features

Dynamic Survey Engine & QR Generation

Administrators can build custom surveys categorized by department (Categories). For each Category, the system auto-generates a downloadable standard/branded QR code.

  • Supports 1-5 Star Ratings and Yes/No boolean questions.
  • Intelligent branching: "Yes" clicks on boolean questions auto-reveal a "Please specify" text input.
  • Seamless mobile-first Guest UI with smooth CSS animations and premium aesthetics.

Composites & Analytics Dashboard

Unstructured 5-star data is mathematically calculated into High-Level Indices using "Composites". The dashboard visualizes these via interactive charts.

  • Line Charts: Daily and Monthly trend analysis of overall averages.
  • Bar/Pie Charts: Drill-down views answering "How did 'Room Cleanliness' perform vs 'Check-in Speed'?"
  • Cross-Category Compare: Place the F&B department side-by-side with the SPA department to identify weak links in the hotel ecosystem.

Real-Time Socket Warnings

Replaces slow email/polling alerts with instant WebSockets. The moment a guest clicks submit on a poor review, admin screens flash.

  • Audio cues (Beep sound) and desktop Toast notifications.
  • Bundled notification dropdown (Warning threshold: rating ≤ 3).
  • Low-Rated Reports view tracks offending reviews alongside the specific guest's Room Number and Phone Number for rapid intervention.

White-Label Customization

A highly configurable hotel settings module allowing individual properties to modify the software to match their distinct brand aesthetic.

  • Upload custom Hotel Logos (Light/Dark variants).
  • Hex-code injection for Application Primary Colors and Accent Colors dynamically updating the guest-facing UI.
  • Injectable Google Review maps URL and custom 'Thank You' messaging.

Yearly Report Export (PDF)

Built-in reporting engine capable of aggregating 12 months of daily and monthly data into a standardized PDF.

  • Organizes selected year tracking across all Composites and individual Questions.
  • Excellent for executive management board meetings and quarterly reviews.

The Data Flow (How It Works)

1

Trigger

Guests scan a physical QR code placed in the Hotel (e.g., table tent in restaurant).

2

Input

Guest fills out the bespoke mobile-optimized survey UI. The system requests optional Room/Phone details.

3

Aggregation

Data hits the MongoDB Backend. Raw scores are averaged against historical data to generate "Composites".

4

Action (The Split)

  • If scores are excellent (4+): Guest is forwarded to Google Reviews to boost public SEO.
  • If scores are poor (≤ 3): Socket.IO emits instant new_notification packet to the Manager Dashboard for service recovery override.
  • Normal Flow: Dashboard metrics update in real-time.
