Kalalak Insight — Complete User Guide

A step-by-step guide to understanding and using the Hotel Department Efficiency Analyzer. Written for first-time users.

📖 1. Introduction

Welcome to Kalalak Insight — your hotel's real-time guest feedback and department analytics platform.

This application allows hotel management teams to collect guest feedback digitally, track department performance across all service areas (Rooms, Food & Beverage, Pool/Leisure, Spa), identify service issues before they escalate, and generate professional reports — all from one centralized dashboard.

📊

Who Is This For?

Hotel administrators, general managers, department heads, and operations teams who need real-time visibility into guest satisfaction.

🎯

What Problem Does It Solve?

Eliminates manual paper-based feedback and spreadsheet reporting. Gives your team instant, department-specific analytics they can act on.

🌐

How Do Guests Submit Feedback?

Guests scan a QR code placed in the hotel room or at the department counter. They fill in a simple form on their phone. No app download needed.

🔐 2. Logging In

The application is accessed from a web browser. No installation is needed.

Application URL: https://insight.kalalak.com

1

Open your web browser

Use any modern browser: Google Chrome, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge. Type the application URL into the address bar and press Enter.

2

Enter your Username

Type the username provided to you by your administrator into the Username field on the login page.

3

Enter your Password

Type your password. Click the eye icon (👁️) to toggle password visibility if needed.

4

Click "Sign In"

You will be redirected to your analytics dashboard. If credentials are wrong, an error message will appear on screen.

ℹ️

Forgot your password? Contact your hotel administrator to reset your account credentials. Staff do not have self-service password reset in this system.

🏠 3. Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you land on the main analytics dashboard. Here is what you will see.

Header Bar (Top)

  • Category Tabs — Switch between departments (Room, F&B, Pool, Spa) at the very top
  • Hotel Name on the left
  • Search — Find composites or questions by name
  • ⚙️ Management — Access the Management section (reports, settings)
  • ⬇️ Download — Download the current chart as PDF
  • 🔔 Notifications — Alerts for low-rated reviews
  • Profile Avatar — Dropdown with account info and logout

Left Sidebar

  • Comp / Ques tabs — Toggle between Composites (grouped scores) and individual Questions
  • Composite names — Click any composite to view its trend chart
  • Question names — Click any question to see its individual rating chart
  • Logout — At the bottom of the sidebar

Main Content Area

  • Summary Stats — Total Reviews, Total Staff, Active Staff at a glance
  • Year Navigator — Use ◀ ▶ arrows to move between years. Current year shows by default.
  • Time Filter — Select Weekly, Monthly, 1 Year, or Custom range
  • Chart Type — Bar chart, Donut chart, or Line chart toggle (top right of chart)
  • The Chart — Visualizes scores for the selected composite or question over time
  • Score Donut — Shows the average score with color coding (red/orange/green)

📊 4. Reading Analytics & Charts

The analytics dashboard shows how guest satisfaction scores have changed over time for each area of your hotel.

Understanding the Main Chart

The bar chart shows average guest ratings (out of 10) for each week, month, or year. Bars are color-coded:

  • 🔴 Red bars — Average score is below 5 (poor performance)
  • 🟠 Orange bars — Average score is 5–7 (average performance)
  • 🟢 Green bars — Average score is above 7 (good performance)

Composites vs. Questions

Composites are grouped scores that combine multiple questions into one metric. For example, "Overall Room Experience" combines Cleanliness, Comfort, and Amenities scores.

  • Click Comp in the sidebar to see grouped performance metrics
  • Click Ques to drill down into individual question scores

Changing What You See

1

Switch Department

Click a category tab at the top (Room Service, Food & Beverage, etc.) to load that department's data.

2

Change Time Period

Click Weekly / Monthly / 1 Year / Custom. Use the arrow buttons to navigate to a previous year (e.g., 2025).

3

Select a Composite or Question

Click any item name in the left sidebar. The chart will refresh to show that specific metric.

4

Compare Analytics

Click the "Compare Analytics" button at the top right of the content area to compare multiple departments or composites side by side.

💡

Tip: Switch to "1 Year" view and go back to 2025 to see the full performance trend — how scores started low when the hotel was new and gradually improved.

🏷️ 5. Category Tabs (Departments)

Each category tab represents a hotel department with its own set of guest review questions and analytics.

  • Room Service — Tracks room cleanliness, comfort, amenities, and staff responsiveness
  • Food & Beverage — Tracks food quality, service speed, menu variety, and restaurant experience
  • Pool / Leisure Area — Tracks pool cleanliness, safety, equipment, and timing
  • Spa — Tracks treatment quality, ambience, therapist professionalism, and booking experience
ℹ️

If you are a Department Head, you will only see the category tabs that your administrator has assigned to your account. Other departments will not appear.

📋 6. Management Reports

The Management section contains specialized reports that go beyond the main dashboard charts. Managers and admins can access these reports.

How to Access Management

1

Click the ⚙️ Management icon

Find the gear icon labeled "Management" in the top header bar, next to the search bar.

2

The Management sidebar opens

A dark panel slides out from the left showing the available report and management links for your role.

3

Navigate to a Report

Click "Yes/No Responses" or "Low Rating Reports" under the REPORTS section of the sidebar.

Yes/No Question Responses

This report shows all guest responses to Yes/No type questions (e.g., "Was the food served at the right temperature?"). Use the date picker to filter the time range and click "Download PDF" to export.

Low-Rated Reviews Report

This report automatically lists all guest reviews where any question received a rating of 3 or below out of 10. Each card shows the guest name, rating, date, and the specific question that was rated low.

⚠️

Only users with the Admin, Viewer, or Department Head role can access these reports. If you don't see the Management link, contact your administrator.

🔔 7. Notifications & Alerts

The notification bell in the top header alerts you when a guest submits a review with unusually low scores.

  • A red badge on the bell icon shows the number of unread alerts
  • Click the bell to open the notification dropdown
  • Each notification shows the review category, rating, and time it was received
  • Click a notification to view the full review details
  • Click "Mark all as read" to clear the badge
💡

Best Practice: Check notifications at the start of each shift. Any rating of 3 or below from the previous day should be reviewed and followed up with the relevant department head.

📥 8. Downloading Reports

You can download the currently visible chart or report as a PDF at any time.

1

Set your desired view

Select the category, composite/question, and time period you want to export before downloading.

2

Click "⬇️ Download" in the header

The download button is visible in the top navigation bar for all authenticated users.

3

Your PDF is saved

The browser will automatically download a PDF file containing the chart and summary data for the selected view.

⭐ 10. Best Practices

Follow these recommendations to use the system efficiently and get the most from your data.

📅

Review Weekly

Set aside 10 minutes every Monday morning. Switch to Weekly view and check each department for any dips in score from the previous week.

🔔

Act on Notifications Quickly

When you receive a low-rating notification, acknowledge it within 24 hours. Flag it to the department head and document the response.

📊

Use Monthly View for Trends

The Monthly view is best for identifying long-term trends and presenting data in management meetings. Switch to 1 Year for the full picture.

🔀

Compare Between Departments

Use the Compare Analytics feature in cross-departmental review meetings to see which teams are excelling and which need support.

📥

Export Before Meetings

Download PDF reports 10 minutes before any management review. This keeps discussions factual and data-driven rather than opinion-based.

🔐

Keep Credentials Secure

Do not share your login credentials with colleagues. Each person should have their own account. Contact your admin to add new users.
