📋 Executive Summary

Kalalak Insight is a comprehensive guest feedback intelligence platform built specifically for hotels and hospitality businesses. It transforms how hotels collect, analyze, and act on guest feedback — enabling continuous service improvement while proactively protecting and strengthening brand reputation through structured service recovery and controlled review amplification logic.

🚀 Key Features

What the Platform Delivers

Every feature engineered for operational clarity and brand protection.

📋

Department-Based Feedback

  • Custom departments (Room, F&B, Reception, Spa, etc.)
  • 1–5 star rating scales for measurable performance
  • Yes/No questions for precise issue detection
📊

Analytics Dashboard

  • Department-level averages and comparisons
  • Date range filtering and trends
  • Composite performance metrics
  • Operational visibility across touchpoints
📄

Professional Reporting

  • Export branded PDF reports
  • Department-level performance summaries
  • Executive-ready analytics documentation
🔔

Service Recovery Alerts

Automatic alerts triggered at 3 stars or below. Enables immediate operational intervention before checkout, significantly reducing the risk of negative public reviews. Includes guest name, phone number, and room number for instant follow-up.

⭐

Reputation Amplification

Guests who submit 4★ or 5★ ratings are invited to leave a public Google review. This structured amplification converts satisfied guests into brand advocates and strengthens booking confidence.
